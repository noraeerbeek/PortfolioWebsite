Projects

Pizza Ordering Website

This website is an interactive and user-friendly platform for ordering pizza. It allows users to select from a variety of delicious pizza options, including Meat Lovers, Hawaiian, and Vegetarian. Customers can customize their orders by adding extra toppings like cheese, sauce, anchovies, pepperoni, and mushrooms to suit their taste. There’s also an option to add drinks like soda, water, or juice to complete the meal. Users can choose between pickup, which is free, or delivery for a small fee, making the service convenient for everyone. The total cost is calculated instantly, and a detailed summary of the order is displayed for review. The design is modern, with vibrant colors, smooth animations, and responsive features that make it look great on all devices. Overall, the website is easy to use and makes ordering pizza a quick and enjoyable experience.

Pacman Game

The Pacman game is a classic maze-chase game where the player navigates Pacman through a maze, collecting pellets while avoiding ghosts. The goal is to clear all the pellets in the maze to progress to the next level. The game includes features like power pellets, which temporarily allow Pacman to eat the ghosts for extra points. The ghosts follow unique movement patterns, adding challenge and strategy to the gameplay. With vibrant visuals and smooth controls, the game offers a fun and engaging experience for players of all ages.As the player advances through levels, the game becomes progressively challenging with faster ghosts and more complex maze layouts. High scores are tracked, encouraging players to beat their previous records and compete with friends. The combination of strategic planning, quick reflexes, and a nostalgic design makes this Pacman game a timeless classic for casual and competitive gamers alike.

Tic Tac Toe Game

The Tic Tac Toe game is a simple yet strategic two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns marking a space with their symbol, either 'X' or 'O'. The objective is to be the first to align three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal to win. If all spaces are filled and neither player achieves this, the game ends in a draw. The game is easy to learn but requires careful thinking and foresight to anticipate the opponent's moves. It promotes logical reasoning and decision-making in a fun and interactive way. This version of the game includes an intuitive user interface for seamless play. With its straightforward rules and engaging gameplay, Tic Tac Toe is a classic favorite for players of all ages.

Beer Brewery Website

This webpage is designed for craft beer enthusiasts, celebrating the rich history and vibrant culture of beer in Ontario. The header features a navigation bar with links to essential pages like Home, Oat House, Blog, and Media, alongside an inviting logo and title. A red section delves into the history of craft beer in Ontario, detailing its evolution from small regional brewers to mass production. The grey section highlights four notable Niagara craft breweries—Brimstone, Ironwood, Oast House, and Exchange—with images and location details. Another red section explores Canada's craft beer renaissance, emphasizing the 1980s resurgence and the pioneers who revolutionized the industry. A grey section provides insights into the future of craft brewing, featuring inspiring quotes from industry leaders. The page uses responsive design and Google Fonts for a modern, polished appearance.
